We reconstructed the gravitational field below \\( z = 0.8 \\) from Cosmicflows-4 distances and supernovae data. The maps yield consistent \\( f\sigma_8 \\) and \\( H_0 \\) values and provide the most detailed publicly released velocity and density fields to date.
