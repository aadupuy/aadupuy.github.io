Cosmicflows-4 compiles distances for over 55,000 galaxies from eight methodologies, anchored to precise local calibrators. The results are consistent with \\( H_0 ≈ 74.6 \text{km s}^{-1}\,\mathrm{Mpc}^{-1} \\) and lay the groundwork for studies of the local velocity field.
