We analyzed three estimators of galaxy peculiar velocity correlations using mock and constrained Cosmicflows-3 datasets. Accounting for observational errors and cosmic variance, we obtained a local measurement of \\( f\sigma_8 = 0.43 \\), consistent with ΛCDM predictions.
